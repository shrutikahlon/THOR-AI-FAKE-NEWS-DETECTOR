About Fake News Detection

📰

What is Fake News?

Fake news refers to deliberately fabricated information that mimics news media content in form but lacks the news media's editorial standards and processes.

🔍

How Detection Works

Our system uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze news content, cross-reference with reliable sources, and identify patterns typical of misinformation.

🛡️

Why It Matters

Detecting fake news helps protect democracy, prevents the spread of harmful misinformation, and maintains public trust in legitimate journalism.
